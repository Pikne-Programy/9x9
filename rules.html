Tic-Tac-Toe 9x9 Rules
  1. The game starts with X playing wherever they want.
  2. Next player can play only in the board that is related to the place where another player put their symbol
  3. When somebody wins on small board (have three in a row) whole board is marked as a victory
  4. When somebody should play on board which is marked as somebody's victory he can play wherever he wants
  5. When somebody has three victories in a row he wins
